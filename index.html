Sues Pizza Store

About

Established in 1985 by Sue Puli, who is originally from Naples, Italy, the birthplace of pizza. Sue's pizza has been offering classic Neapolitan pizza for over 2 decades.

Whether hungry for a traditional slice of Margherita or one of our contemporary classics, Sue has got you covered.

For more information, orders or catering, get in touch: info@suespizza.store or call: 803 266-7451 for collection & local delivery.
